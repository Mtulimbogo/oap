Before application

Before beginning an application please read the Application instructions carefully. Application documents shall be submitted in English. It is the candidate’s responsibility to ensure that the application is complete, including referee assessments.
All Referee assessment and recommendation letter, the National ID Number should be referenced as the Identification and should be verified by the referee.

Application Process
  1. Complete all tasks listed for the Programme application checklist.
  2. Print the entire application.
  3. Save the PDF version of the entire, completed application documents.
  4. Submit the completed electronic application through submission-email.
  5. It is the candidate’s responsibility to make sure that the referee’s assessments and recommendation letters reaches the Secretariat. The Secretariat will inform the candidate by email once received.
  6. For technical help, call or email from 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. East African Time.
  7. For programme-related help, please contact email egovdidc@ega.go.tz

Complete application
  1. Application details (form pages)
  2. Recent updated CV
  3. Nomination letter – maximum two pages (signed by the relevant department head of the nominating institution)
  4. Institution transcripts (scanned and attached by the institution’s administrative staff)
  5. Personal Motivation Statement – maximum two pages (self-evaluation written by the applicant)
  6. Two reference Letters – maximum two pages each (written by someone who knows the candidate in a non-academic capacity – this letter is attached to the nomination by the candidate)
  7. Research proposal – maximum two pages (written by the applicant)
  8. Project references – maximum five pages (written by the applicant)
  9. Research contributions – maximum one page (written by the applicant)
